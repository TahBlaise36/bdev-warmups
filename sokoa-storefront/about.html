Our story

We built Sokoa so buying local feels easy.

From a phone counter in Buea to a marketplace serving thousands — Sokoa connects you with sellers you can trust, payment you already use, and delivery right to your door.

What we believe

Built for how Cameroon actually shops

Local sellers first

Every product comes from a real shop or stall in Buea or Douala. We give small sellers a storefront that competes with anyone.

Pay the way you pay

MTN MoMo, Orange Money, or cash on delivery. No card required, no hidden steps — checkout in the currency and method you already trust.

Delivered to your door

Riders who know the neighbourhoods. Most orders in Buea and Douala arrive the same day — and it's free over 25,000 XAF.
